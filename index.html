I am PhD student in the Department of Automatic Control & Systems Engineering at The University of Sheffield, in the post-submission phase of my doctorate. I am based at Sheffield Robotics, and I am supervised by Dr. Roderich Gross and Prof. Tony J. Dodd.

My research focuses on self-reconfigurable modular robotics, specifically the development of a new system, called HyMod. To find out more about this research, please visit the publications page. During my spare time, I have been involved in a number of hardware and software projects, either by myself or as part of a team. Details of these can be found on the projects page.
